Latitude vs. Max Temperature

This is a scatterplot visualization that shows the correlation between city latitude and maximum temperature. The plot indicates a positive linear relationship between the two variables. The highest temperatures are recorded between latitudes 0 and 20, which are close to the equator. As the latitude approaches the poles at 80, the maximum temperature decreases.
